I want to be a member!

You are a few steps away from joining IEEE and our student branch. To do so you have to follow a little procedure:

  1. Register here
    1. Choose Universidade do Porto on University/College
    2. The membership cost is about 27$
    3. This small cost gives you free access to workshops and events sponsored by IEEE and access to room J201 at FEUP
  2. Send an email to {{site.email}} with the following information:
    1. Name
    2. IEEE number given after the registration
    3. Mobile phone number
    4. University of Porto number

After checking your information we will send you an email confirming your registration. We will also start the process to give you access to room J201.

If you have any doubts, don't be afraid to send us an email to {{site.email}}.
