IEEE Women in Engineering
University of Porto

WiE is the largest professional organization devoted to the promotion of women in the fields of engineering and science.

The main objective of WiE is to attain equal opportunities between genders, terminate the bias of some areas of engineering and science and encourage, from an early age, women to join these fields of work.
