IEEE Women in Engineering UP


WiE (Women in Engineering) is the largest international professional organization dedicated to promoting women in the fields of cience and engineering.
WiE UP aims to represent WiE in Universidade do Porto, promoting events and activities mostly directed to people who study on those fields.

IEEE EMBS SB Chapter UP


IEEE Engineering in Medicine and Biology Society (EMBS) is the largest international organization of biomedical engineers.
Recently created, EMBS SB Chapter UP aims to enrich the base formation, to develop small projects, organize workshops and competitions, to participate on national and international conferences and to be able to bring speakers and prominent personalities in the field to the university.
